Quest for Zariba

A strategy game about risk-based financing and health services in developing countries

The Game

Quest for Zariba is a time-based strategy game, based in the developing country of Zariba. Play as a Health Control coordinator, traveling throughout the land to learn about the people and the problems. Then, use risk-based financing to plan and impliment services that will restore Zariba to glory!

The World

Zariba is a timeless land, filled with unique people and struggles. The world may seem similar to some a generalized developing country, stylized to identify with no one country, and yet all of them.

The Problems

Zariba has been dealing with many years of poverty, drought, political and social problems, among many others. To be short, the country is in a bad state. Speak to people throughout the country, traveling to different cities to find out what the most serious problems are. One of the main problems in Zariba is the transportation system, however, and you only have so many days to talk to as many citizens as possible.

Your Role

Since Zariba is filled with all of these serious issues, debilitating the people and the country's systems, they really need some help. As the Health Control coordinator assigned to Zariba, your job is to set up a plan to solve these problems. As you speak to people, different options will become unlocked in your journal. Put together these potential solutions however you see fit, attempting to make a plan that will solve the most problems and help Zariba rise from the ashes.
