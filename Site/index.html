Quest for Zariba

A strategy game about risk-based financing and health services in developing countries

The Game

Quest for Zariba is a time-based strategy game, based in the developing country of Zariba. Play as a Health Control coordinator, traveling throughout the land to learn about the people and the problems. Then, use risk-based financing to plan and impliment services that will restore Zariba to glory!
