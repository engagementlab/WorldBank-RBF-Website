Welcome to Zariba!

Explore the world of Zariba by unlocking routes that will take you to each of Zariba's cities. Construct an RBF proposal that will help raise the country's health indicators. Take your plan, or another player's plan, and implement it on a wider scale, adjusting and monitoring the program over the course of three simulated years. Download the game client below for Mac OS or Windows to start your journey!
